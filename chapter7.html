7.
Impact
&
Legacy

Piet Mondrian died of pneumonia
in 1944, at the age of 71.

Mondrian is renowned as an early pioneer of abstraction in painting,
as well as a fundamental advocate of modernist ideology in the arts.

His minimalism, geometry, and compositional brilliance
have had an immense impact on the modern and post-modern art world.

His style has influenced design and fashion,
and even found references within pop culture.
